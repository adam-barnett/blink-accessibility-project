welcome:Welcome to blink-able. This Procedure will initialise the system.
find rotation:Please try to keep still throughout this process and look at the screen.  Currently the system is finding your face.
capture eyes:Now please blink several times so that your eyes can be captured.
finished:Success, your eyes have been captured.
no blink:The system did not detect a clear blink. Please blink again now.
no rotation:The system was not able to find a face in the camera, check the lighting and positioning. The system will try again in 30 seconds.
no camera:Apologies, the camera was either not found or connection to it was lost.  Closing down.
